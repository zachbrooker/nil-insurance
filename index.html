Why World

What separates specialty from general-lines.

NIL and performance-based insurance capacity lives in a narrow corner of the London market. The program structure has to reflect how your contracts actually work — not the other way around.

  1. / 01

    Direct Lloyd's of London access

    Through Bretton Woods International — the London-based Lloyd's broker acquired by our wholesale arm Novatae Risk Group in 2024 — placements reach the specialty syndicates that actually underwrite contingency and performance-based risk.

  2. / 02

    Specialty, not adapted

    NIL coverage is not a repurposed general-liability policy. Program terms mirror your underlying NIL contracts, injury provisions, and payment schedules — so coverage tracks with the way your program is actually obligated.

  3. / 03

    Former Division I athletes on the specialty team

    Zach Brooker (USC Basketball) and Malcolm MacLean (Bucknell Lacrosse) both competed as Division I student-athletes. That shows up in how we think about roster dynamics, injury risk, and contract structure.

  4. / 04

    Institutional backing

    World Insurance Associates is co-backed by Goldman Sachs Asset Management and Charlesbank Capital Partners, and ranked among Business Insurance's 100 Largest Brokers of U.S. Business.
